Homemade Ice Cream

Use an electric ice cream make to make delicious ice cream at home. The possibility in flavours is endless!

  • Ice cream machine
  • Heavy cream
  • Cane sugar
  • Whole milk
  • Pinch of salt
  • Eggs optional
  1. Combine ingredients in a saucepan
  2. Whisk on low-medium heat, stirring every 5 minutes
  3. Pour ingredients in machine, preset, and enjoy!
